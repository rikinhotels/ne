Navkar Engineering Company is a leading manufacturer of all types of Pipe Fittings viz. M.S. Pipe Fittings, S.S. Pipe Fittings, etc, catering to the industries like Pharma, Oil & Gas, Manufacturing, Chemical and Dairy Industries.
Our dedication and commitment towards the quality of our products, maintenance of our plant, higher productivity and trained and skilled Human Resources has helped to us sustain for more than 15 years in the industry.
Under the guidance of our Managing Director Shri Rajesh Jain and our highly experienced HR and Marketing team, we have been able to satisfy our customers, with timely delivery of quality products.
Vision:
Our vision is to become the leading company in S.S & Pipe-Fitting products and related businesses. Navkar engg is committed to provide quality products to its customers as per standard and best suited to local environment at affordable price.
Mission:

1. To exercise maximum care to manufacture quality product by employing a team of highly skilled technicians and professional managers.

2. To redress customers complaints promptly.

3. To utilize optimum resources through good corporate governance for better servicing.

4. To increase productivity through improved human resources.
